--- title: "Water Texture Development" excerpt: "A 3D driving game where players navigate city streets in a school bus to pick up students and deliver them to school while avoiding obstacles and racing against the clock.
" collection: portfolio --- Problem: To explore a new application of graphics programming. Using reflection and refraction mapping to simulate the water and apply it to a flat surface with texture mixture. Approach: Utilize multiple frame buffer objects and clipping planes to generate textures that display geometry above and below the water surface, project those textures onto a flat plane, and apply a number of effects to make the texture appear more like realistic, flowing water.

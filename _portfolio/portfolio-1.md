Project Description: 3D combat racing game where players compete as drivers, racing through hazardous tracks against opponents and checkpoint timers. Players must manage durability and popularity by performing tricks and crashing into rivals until they are the last racer remaining. 

Approach: Developed the HUD systems in Unity and C#, including the speedometer, checkpoint
timer, health and boost meters, and directional indicators. Collaborated with artists to integrate UI
assets and implemented track hazards, including an artillery projectile system.

Wow the crowd with flips, drifts, and vehicular carnage in Dread Rally, an arcade racing game where your life is in the audience’s hands. Welcome to Dread Rally, a 3D arcade racing game where you must outlast 11 other racers in order to become the people’s champion! While racing you must entertain the audience with fancy flip tricks and the destruction of your opponents all while trying to go as fast as possible. Select your vehicle from 3 factions: Brute Corp, Esotechnica, and A-Star. Play solo or with a friend in couch co-op through 4 tracks set on Mars during the aftermath of a corporate war between rival industries.

Dread Rally was a senior capstone project in which I worked as part of an interdisciplinary team to develop and release a video game. Dread Rally is a racing style game in which you have to avoid hazards and destroy you rivals as you race around the track at high speeds.
